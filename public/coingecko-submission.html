2. Descripción del Proyecto

¿De qué trata el proyecto?

AGROVIDA (AGRO) is a deflationary ERC-20 utility token built on Polygon that powers a health and wellness ecosystem in Latin America. The token enables users to purchase healthy food products, superfoods, supplements, and wellness services through the AGROVIDA marketplace. Every token transfer automatically burns 2% of the amount, creating a deflationary mechanism that reduces total supply over time. AGROVIDA originated from Colombian blueberry farming and has expanded into a multi-category health platform connecting producers, consumers, and wellness professionals.

¿Qué hace único al proyecto?

AGROVIDA combines a real physical commerce network with blockchain technology. Unlike purely digital tokens, AGROVIDA is backed by an active e-commerce platform (agrovidacol.com) with physical distribution points, smart refrigeration units, and a delivery network in Colombia. The 2% automatic burn on every transfer creates sustained deflation. The ecosystem rewards buyers with AGROVIDA tokens on purchases, creating a circular economy between consumers, producers, delivery partners, and health professionals.

Historia del proyecto

AGROVIDA was founded in Colombia with roots in blueberry farming and agricultural production. The project evolved from a traditional food distribution business into a blockchain-powered health and wellness ecosystem. The company, registered as Agroblockchain, developed an e-commerce platform (agrovidacol.com) with physical distribution infrastructure including smart refrigeration units and delivery networks across Colombia. In 2026, the AGROVIDA token was launched on the Polygon blockchain as a utility token to tokenize the existing commercial ecosystem and reward participants in the network.

¿Qué sigue para el proyecto?

AGROVIDA plans to expand its physical distribution network across Latin America, targeting Mexico, Peru, and Chile in the next 12 months. The roadmap includes the launch of NFT memberships that grant access to exclusive products and discounts, a staking program offering yield to long-term token holders, and a DAO governance system allowing token holders to vote on product offerings and ecosystem decisions.

¿Para qué se puede usar el token?

AGROVIDA token serves as the primary medium of exchange within the AGROVIDA ecosystem. Token holders can use AGROVIDA to purchase healthy food products, superfoods, supplements, and wellness services on the agrovidacol.com platform. The token also provides access to NFT memberships with tiered benefits, staking rewards for long-term holders, and governance voting rights in the project DAO.
